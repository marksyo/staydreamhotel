2. How to check-in

(1) Submit a copy of your passport image by one day before check-in

Under Japanese inn law, it is mandatory to record passport information for foreign travelers. Please send a photo image of your passport by one day before check-in.
Personal information obtained from you will be properly managed and will not be provided, disclosed, etc., to third parties other than with your consent, except as required by law.

(2) Obtain the key from the key box and unlock the front door. Gently Shift the left door from left to right.

Unlock: Lightly press the left door to the left, Turn the key to the right
Lock: Lightly press the left door to the left, Turn the key to the left

Please gently, gently, gently shift the left door to the right to open the door.

Please keep the key box inside the facility.
