  • 2. Entire facility is non-smoking (including balconies)

House Rules (Full Text)

Stay Dream Hotel (hereinafter referred to as "the facility") has established the following house rules to ensure the safety and comfort of our guests. If these rules are not followed, we reserve the right to refuse accommodation or use of the facility. Please note that the facility will not be responsible for the loss of guests' property or safety that is not the responsibility of the facility. In the event of facility unavailability due to natural disasters, disasters, incidents, or equipment failures, we will refund the charges for the unusable days, but we will not compensate for other losses (such as transportation expenses).

1. Facility Usage

  • (1) No shoes allowed inside the facility, except on balconies. Please do not go outside wearing indoor slippers to maintain cleanliness.
  • (2) Evacuation can be done through the 1st-floor entrance and windows of 1st-floor rooms.
  • (3) Always lock the door when in the room or especially during sleep.
  • (4) The entire facility, including balconies, is non-smoking. Smoking inside the facility incurs a penalty of 50,000 yen.
  • (5) Accommodate only the number of guests reserved. Unauthorized guests, different numbers of guests, or stays are prohibited.
  • (6) The facility's contracted amperage is a maximum of 60A. The maximum amperage for a single electrical outlet is 15A. Proper electrical usage is essential to avoid power outages.
  • (7) Close all windows and doors throughout the facility before going out to prevent rain from entering in case of sudden weather changes.
  • (8) Do not use guest rooms for business purposes (exhibitions, offices, etc.) without permission.
  • (9) No loud parties are allowed, especially between 9 PM and 7 AM, out of consideration for neighbors.
  • (10) Pets are not allowed.

2. Entrance Door Key

  • (1) Always lock the facility when going out during your stay.
  • (2) Do not disclose the entrance door key of the facility to anyone other than registered guests.
  • (3) Keep the entrance door key safe. Losing it incurs a high cost of over 100,000 yen.

3. Payment and Related Matters

  • (1) The facility only accepts Japanese Yen in cash. Please use credit cards for reservations through Airbnb or other OTA sites.
  • (2) Additional charges and penalties will be invoiced in cash.
  • (3) Please make the payment by the checkout date.

4. Anti-Social Forces, Concerns about Violating Public Order and Morality

If a facility user is deemed to fall under any of the following, we will promptly refuse usage and ask for immediate departure. Note that if such facts are discovered after booking or during usage, we will refuse usage at that time.

  • (1) In the event of being identified as a member of an organized crime group, a person related to an organized crime group, or any other anti-social forces.
  • (2) In case of violence, threats, extortion, unduly demanding and similar acts are recognized.
  • (3) Individuals who have been notified of usage refusal by the facility in the past.
  • (4) In cases of engaging in acts that disrupt morals, such as gambling, or causing inconvenience to neighbors.
  • (5) In situations where there is a risk of endangering oneself or causing feelings of danger, fear, or anxiety to the neighborhood due to mental exhaustion, drugs, alcohol-induced loss of self-control, etc.
  • (6) If there are acts such as loud singing, shouting, or disruptive behavior inside or near the facility that causes disgust or inconvenience to others.
  • (7) In the case of acts similar to the above.

5. Prohibited Items and Actions

If the following prohibited actions are discovered, we will promptly refuse usage, and you will be asked to leave the facility. A penalty of over 300,000 yen will be charged.

  • (1) Prohibited Items to Bring In
  • ・ Animals, birds, and other pets
  • ・ Gunpowder, volatile oils, and other inflammable or explosive items
  • ・ Items emitting foul odors
  • ・ Items prohibited from possession by law or regulations
  • (2) Prohibited Actions
  • ・ Distribution of advertisements, promotional materials, selling items, and solicitation without permission from the facility
  • ・ Taking photos, videos, or conducting any business-related activities without permission from the facility
  • ・ Smoking outside designated smoking areas within the facility and its nearby surroundings

6. Changes to House Rules

  • (1) The facility may, at its discretion, change these rules.
  • (2) In the event of changes to these rules, the facility will publish the changes, including the content of the new rules and their effective date, on the facility's website at least one week before the effective date.
  • (3) If a guest uses the services of the facility based on these rules after the effective date of the changed rules, it will be deemed that the guest has agreed to the changes in these rules.
  • (4) The interpretation and effectiveness of these rules shall be in accordance with Japanese law.

Established on May 1, 2023

Cancellation Policy

  • (1) The cancellation policy of the facility is free until 7 days before check-in. Cancellation fees are 50% for 5 days before, 80% for 2 days before, and 100% on the day of check-in.
  • (2) For Airbnb, free cancellation is until 4:00 PM, 5 days before. Afterward, a 50% refund (excluding the first night and service fee) will be provided.
  • (3) Cancellation policies for other booking sites are as listed on those sites.
  • (4) In the case of long-term stays, charges are billed in advance, and there is no refund for mid-term cancellations.
Cancellation fees vary by OTA site, so please follow the cancellation policy of the OTA site you booked with.
